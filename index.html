Dora's Study Corner! 📚 BIDMAS Basics: Hey Dora, BIDMAS is like a secret code for solving math puzzles! It stands for Brackets, Indices, Division/Multiplication, Addition/Subtraction. We follow this order to get the right answer every time! 🎉 Brackets -> () First, do what's inside! Indices -> x² or 8² Like powers! Division -> ÷ or fractions Multiplication -> × Addition -> + Subtraction -> - Example: 2 + 3 × 4. Multiply first: 3 × 4 = 12, then add: 2 + 12 = 14. Easy peasy! 😊 Back to Progress

  1. 1. Calculate the following using the correct order of operations.
    • a. 6 + 3 × 4
      Hey Dora! Let's solve 6 + 3 × 4 step by step using BIDMAS. First, no brackets or indices. Next, multiplication: 3 × 4 = 12. Then addition: 6 + 12 = 18. Remember, multiply before add! You're doing great! 🌟
    • b. 18 - 12 / 3
      Awesome, Dora! For 18 - 12 / 3: No brackets/indices. Division first: 12 / 3 = 4. Then subtract: 18 - 4 = 14. Division before subtraction! Keep going! 💪
    • c. 8 + (-10)
      You got this, Dora! 8 + (-10) is like 8 - 10 = -2. Just addition with negative, no other operations to worry about. Simple! 😄
    • d. 17 - 3 + (-8)
      Super! 17 - 3 + (-8): All addition/subtraction, so left to right. 17 - 3 = 14, 14 + (-8) = 14 - 8 = 6. Negatives are tricky, but you nailed it! 🏆
  2. 2. Evaluate the following expressions using the correct order of operations.
    • a. 6 × (-9)
      Dora, let's do 6 × (-9): Just multiplication with negative: 6 × -9 = -54. No other steps! You're a math hero! ⚡
    • b. 72 × 8 - 7
      Great job! 72 × 8 - 7: Multiply first: 72 × 8 = 576, then subtract: 576 - 7 = 569. BIDMAS rules! 🎈
    • c. 7 + (-1) - 4
      Fun one! 7 + (-1) - 4: Left to right: 7 - 1 = 6, 6 - 4 = 2. Handling negatives like a pro! 😎
    • d. (6 + 3) / 9
      Yay! (6 + 3) / 9: Brackets first: 6 + 3 = 9, then divide: 9 / 9 = 1. Brackets are key! 🔑
  3. 3. Calculate the following expressions using the correct order of operations. (only a and d)
    • a. -12 + 2 × (-1)
      Cool, Dora! -12 + 2 × (-1): Multiply first: 2 × -1 = -2, then add: -12 + -2 = -14. Multiples before adds! 🌈
    • d. (4 + 8 × 7) - 7
      Exciting! (4 + 8 × 7) - 7: Inside brackets, multiply first: 8 × 7 = 56, then 4 + 56 = 60. Then 60 - 7 = 53. BIDMAS in action! 🚀
  4. 7. The expression 6 + 2 × (5 - 10) / 2 is equal to:
    a. -15 b. -35 c. -60 d. 1
    Adventure time! 6 + 2 × (5 - 10) / 2: Brackets: 5 - 10 = -5. Then multiply: 2 × -5 = -10. Divide: -10 / 2 = -5. Add: 6 + -5 = 1. So d. 1! Well done! 🥳
  5. 9. A submarine dives 100m below sea level, rises 60m, then dives 25m. Calculate its final position.
    Underwater quest! Start at 0. Dives 100m: -100. Rises 60m: -100 + 60 = -40. Dives 25m: -40 - 25 = -65m. Negative means below sea level! 🌊
  6. 12. Darcy says there is no difference between the expressions 8 - 2 × (4 + 2) and 8 - 2 × 4 + 2. Do you agree with Darcy? Justify your answer with correct working.
    Detective Dora! First: 8 - 2 × (4 + 2). Brackets: 4 + 2 = 6. Multiply: 2 × 6 = 12. Subtract: 8 - 12 = -4.
    Second: 8 - 2 × 4 + 2. Multiply: 2 × 4 = 8. Then 8 - 8 + 2 = 2. Different! So no, brackets change everything! 🕵️‍♀️
  7. 15. Ryan buys a vanilla cupcake at $3.75, 2 cinnamon donuts at $5.25 each, and 3 chocolate chip cookies at $2.50 each. Determine the total amount Ryan has to pay.
    Yummy math! Cupcake: 3.75. Donuts: 2 × 5.25 = 10.50. Cookies: 3 × 2.50 = 7.50. Total: 3.75 + 10.50 + 7.50 = 21.75. Multiply quantities first! 🍩
